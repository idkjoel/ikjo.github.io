Better, together.

So often, we humans wander through our lives passively, either not realizing or not caring about the impacts our decisions have on not only our own lives, but the lives of those around us. The truth is that every decision and every action we choose is a vote for the type of people we hope to become and the type of world we hope to live in. I aspire to work on projects and teams that are focused on making meaningful contributions through purposeful, intentional choices, and to contribute to a better world -- as a designer and as a human being.
