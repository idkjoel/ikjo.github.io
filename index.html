Hi! My name is Joel, and I'm a User Experience Designer. I build meticulously crafted, human-centred products and digital offerings that prioritize simple, functional, and satisfying experiences.

Formally Educated

Currently in my third year of studies at Wilfrid Laurier University, I'm working towards an Honours Bachelor of Design in User Experience Design (the first of its kind in Canada).

Industry Experienced

I've been fortunate to work with partners, clients, and companies in a variety of roles over the years; I have the real-world experience needed to step right in and get to work.

Feedback Driven

Iterative, human-centred design is the core of what I do; I solve problems collaboratively, working with multiple stakeholders to make sure things get done in a way that works for everyone.
