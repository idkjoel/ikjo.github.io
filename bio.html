Hi, I'm Joel!

My name is Joel, I'm a UX student from Canada doing his best to make things better.

I work really hard on making sure I am contributing to a world which has become so increasingly full of content. We live in an age where there are many forms of content to be consumed, an in this age of infinite consumption I hope to contribute some content of my own.

I read Steve Job's biography in the eigth grade, and although it has it's critics it is the story of a man who's only goal was to make things better. Although I didn't immediately realize it, that book was a turning point in my life.

This book woke up something inside of me; Steve's dedication and commitment to excellence was captivating to me. Making his engineers sign the inside of the Mac... I realized that to be an artist, you didn't need to put a brush to a canvas. It's more than that.

And so here I am, doing my best to improve on interactions that happen every day; with our phones, our friends, and our environment. Realizing I have an opprotunity to do this so many various ways is what motivates me to be better.

LET'S WORK TOGETHER
