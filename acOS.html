acOS

Abandoned

home/portfolio/acOS

DETAILS

Developed: 2015 - 2016

iOS: v1.1


acOS was my second attempt to make an all-encompassing theme for iOS. There were certain design elements of iOS that frustrated me when looking at them, and the icons were the place to start. The lack of consistency in colour, the lack of character or design direction went beyond the icons and into the actual OS at an application level, and acOS was something that I wanted to do to try to combat that.

Unfortunately, I never really carried out my grand plans the way I was hoping to, and although I got a beautiful set of icons I never really got the deeper level of integration that I was looking for. I've since moved on to other attempts to build off of the principles of this project, but this project is no longer being updated to support future versions of iOS.
